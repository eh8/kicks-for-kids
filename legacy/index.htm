About

Headed and founded by Alex Yang, Jake Therrien and Robert Park, Kicks for Kids is a nonprofit organization that strives to give kids in Jamaica an opportunity to play soccer with proper equipment.

Our club obtains most of its supplies through fundraisers and equipment drives ranging from shin guards to cleats. Our organization sponsors Cleats and Stuff Jamaica, which helps directly give kids in Jamaica soccer gear.
